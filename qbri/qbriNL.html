QScience.com celebrates Open Access Week

As part of International Open Access Week, QScience held an event to increase awareness of open access publishing. Representatives from Open Researcher and Contributor ID (ORCID), Georgetown University in Qatar, and Arab Initiatives of Open Access (AIOA) outlined the benefits of OA publishing and its potential expansion in the Middle East, the event also addressed the issue of providing more open access repositories, where research content is freely available. Dr Christopher Leonard, Editorial Director, also presented at Georgetown University in Qatar earlier in the week on Gold Open Access Publishing.

Dr. Alwaleed AlKhaja, Senior Commissioning Editor, has been chosen as editor for DOAJ, the Directory of Open Access Journals, where he will work with a team of associate editors to assist in the process of selecting Arabic content journals to go into database. DOAJ is regarded as an authoritative registry of OA journals which meet international standards.

Dr. Alwaleed AlKhaja, also presented at the Sultan Qaboos SLA-AGC Medical Information Symposium in Muscat, Oman. His presentation was on the topic of Open Access Biomedical Publications in the GCC. The audience largely comprised of active researchers and practicing medics in Oman.
